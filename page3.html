INTERCONNECTIONS

Meiosis and gametogenesis go hand in hand.

Gametogenesis is the production of gametes, and meiosis is a type of cell division specifically for creating sex cells, gametes. One cannot be used without the other in us humans, it's how we reproduce. While meiosis and gametogenesis may just be small pieces of a big human puzzle in actuality,  they are no less important for they complete natural instinct; to reproduce.
